About the Hackathon

Get ready for a two-day innovation sprint where imagination meets impact!
Hosted by AUS in collaboration with the Sharjah Commerce & Tourism Development Authority (SCTDA), this hackathon brings together the UAE’s boldest thinkers to redefine the future of sustainable tourism. Over the course of the event, teams will develop their solutions from concept to prototype with guidance from industry mentors and experts.
It’s not just a competition - it’s a launchpad for ideas that move the world.

  • Empower Emerging Talent: Build and refine working prototypes with expert mentorship throughout the hackathon.
  • Innovation with Purpose: Align innovation with the UAE’s goals for smarter, more sustainable tourism and development.
  • Recognition & Exposure: Pitch your final solution on the Sharjah International Forum 2025 stage to leaders, investors, and innovators.

Expected Submissions from Participants

Shortlisted teams must deliver a 5-minute presentation of their solution or idea, presented in one or more of the following formats:

  • Concept Pitch: A well-structured idea supported by visuals, research insights, and an implementation roadmap (e.g., wireframes, mockups, or user journey flows).
  • Working MVP / Prototype: A live demo or proof of concept demonstrating technical feasibility — even with minimal functionality, practical execution earns higher recognition.
  • Data Visualizations: Use of charts, graphs, or infographics to illustrate the challenge, insights, and potential outcomes.
  • Short Video Pitch: A brief 1–3 minute video summarizing the idea, its value, and real-world impact.
  • UX/UI Prototypes: Interactive visuals or design mockups showcasing user experience, interface flow, and functionality.

All submissions should present integrated, practical, and high-impact solutions that align with the UAE’s vision for a smart and sustainable future.
