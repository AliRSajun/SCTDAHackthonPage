About the Hackathon

Get ready for a two-day innovation sprint where imagination meets impact! Hosted by AUS in collaboration with the Sharjah Commerce & Tourism Development Authority (SCTDA), this hackathon brings together the UAE’s boldest thinkers to redefine the future of sustainable tourism.

  • Empower Emerging Talent: Build working prototypes with expert mentorship.
  • Innovation with Purpose: Align creativity with the UAE’s sustainability goals.
  • Recognition & Exposure: Pitch on the Sharjah International Travel and Tourism Forum stage to leaders and investors.

Who Can Participate

The Sharjah Tourism Innovation Hackathon is open to all undergraduate students enrolled in universities across the UAE - regardless of major or discipline.

Whether you’re studying engineering, design, business, data science, or the arts, this hackathon welcomes every perspective that can help shape the future of sustainable tourism. Participants are encouraged to form interdisciplinary teams combining creativity, technical expertise, and strategic thinking.

  • Eligibility: Undergraduate students at any UAE university.
  • Team Size: 4 members.
  • Selection: Participants will be shortlisted based on registration responses.

Expected Submissions from Participants

At the end of the hackathon, teams must deliver a 5-minute presentation of their solution or idea presented in one or more of the following formats:
💡
Concept Pitch
Idea with visuals, research, and roadmap.
⚙️
Working MVP / Prototype
Functional demo, even if minimal.
📊
Data Visualizations
Charts or infographics showing insights and outcomes.
🎥
Short Video Pitch
1–3 minute summary of the idea and impact.
🖥️
UX/UI Prototypes
Interactive or visual mockups demonstrating interface and flow.

All submissions should present integrated, practical, and high-impact solutions that align with the UAE’s vision for a smart and sustainable future.
